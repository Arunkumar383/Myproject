NADI REMEDIES

In Nadi Astrology, guidance on remedies often faces varying levels of commitment. Some are inspired to perform pujas, feed the needy, support orphans, donate to temples, or establish charities. This reflects the karmic guidance and divine rewards based on past virtuous deeds. Every soul carries the consequences of past actions—good deeds bring benefits, while bad deeds lead to hardships. The aim is to avoid evil and embrace good, as life's joys and sorrows are determined by past actions. Understanding the Shanti Chapter in Nadi Astrology reveals past life karmas and offers solutions and remedies. By practicing good deeds daily, one can reap the rewards of a peaceful and fulfilling life.

1. SHANTI REMEDY– First Path According to the first path, Nadi Astrology seeks to identify the miseries in our lives, and as a remedy for our evil deeds, we must perform the opposite act of mercy where the miseries are. Have you been celibate for a long time after reaching marriageable age in your life?

2.SHANTI PARIKARAM-The Second Path According to the second path of Shanti Parikaram, building a temple, installing a deity, and promoting kindness to others can mitigate the effects of past-life sins. For instance, someone facing obstacles in marriage due to sarpathosha (snake dosha) can worship daily at a temple, offer charity to the needy, and thereby dissolve past negative karma. These actions pave the way for marriage and happiness. The aim is to inspire others on the path of righteousness, helping them overcome past misdeeds and experience positive transformations in life through divine guidance. Following the teachings of Shantikandam as advised by the Guru ensures significant changes in one's life.

3.PEACE REMEDIES-The Third Path According to the third path of Shanti Parikaram, mitigating the effects of past-life sins involves faithfully chanting original mantras, performing pujas, feeding the needy, aiding orphans, donating to temples, and establishing charities. Neglecting this path and harboring negative thoughts may lead to unfavorable outcomes. Effective remedial measures can yield desired results within days, depending on factors like past karma, sincere penance, consulting a genuine astrologer, and one's lifestyle.
